A Little Bit about Kayla

I am Kayla Fletcher, a current High School student who attends Gettys  D. Broome High School. This webpage will introduce you to some aspects of my life and let you get to know me better! 

Volleyball 

    Most of my time outside of school is spent on volleyball. I've played volleyball since 7th grade and am now in my senior season of volleyball. I play Right side hitter for Broome High School. We have practice everyday, games on Tuesdays and Thursdays, and some tournaments on the weekends. 

This is a picture of me playing volleyball in the middle of a game and diving for a ball. 

Part-time Job

I currently work part-time job at American Eagle at the mall as a sales associate. I have been working there since march 2021, but recently I am taking a break due to the volleyball season and I will return to work in January of 2022. 

Some Facts about me

My birthday is September 23, 2003. I will turn 18 this year. My favorite color is purple, and my favorite animal is a butterfly. My favorite type of food is Italian and I am the oldest girl out of my two younger brothers and my three cousins. When I was younger I was very clumsy and cut my eyelid open on a doorknob.  

My Future Plans 

I plan to graduate from Broome Highschool in May 2022. After graduation, I plan to attend college to eventually get my Master's in nursing to become a nurse practitioner. However, I really want to become a pediatric nurse practitioner. Some colleges that I am considering are USC, Baylor, Vanderbilt, College of Charleston, and Alabama.
